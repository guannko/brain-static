FAQ

Find answers to frequently asked questions about our pricing and plans.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect on your next billing cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

No, there are no setup fees or hidden charges. You only pay the monthly subscription price.

Can I cancel anytime?

Absolutely! You can cancel your subscription at any time with no cancellation fees.

What counts as an AI check?

Each analysis of your brand across AI systems counts as one check. Reports and monitoring don't count extra.

Do you offer annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing gives you 2 months free (pay for 10 months, get 12).

Is there a free trial?

Our Free plan lets you test the platform with 5 checks per month, no credit card required.
